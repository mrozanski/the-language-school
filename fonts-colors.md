# Fonts and Colors

## Initial visual direction:
Warm, confident, and human. Clean and readable, with subtle personality.

## Colors:
Charcoal text on warm off-white backgrounds, with calm teal for primary actions and muted clay accents for warmth.

## Typography:
Inter for body and UI (clarity and multilingual support), paired with a serif headline font to convey credibility and trust.
